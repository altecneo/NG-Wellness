Founder's Story

A Practice Built on Passion & Purpose

My passion for functional wellness grew from both professional experience and personal health challenges.

After years of helping clients navigate complex health concerns, I became increasingly convinced that many people need more than symptom management. They need someone willing to listen, investigate, educate, and help identify potential underlying contributors to their health concerns.

Beauty for Broken Wellness was created to provide a personalized, client-centered approach that helps individuals understand their bodies and make informed decisions about their wellness.

"I believe true wellness involves caring for the whole person — body, mind, and spirit."

— Julie Miles, ND

My Philosophy

Healing is rarely about finding a single answer. It often involves uncovering patterns, addressing imbalances, reducing stress, supporting foundational health, and making sustainable lifestyle changes that promote long-term wellness.
